How it works

Three steps to knowing your zip.

1

Set your ZIP

Sign up with your phone, verify your identity, and set your home ZIP via GPS or manual entry.

2

Post or browse

Share what's happening in your neighborhood. Or browse any ZIP in the country to see what locals are saying.

3

Stay in the know

Get real-time alerts for replies, mentions, and nearby posts. Never miss what's happening around you again.
